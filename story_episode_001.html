Fruit Crush Villa Episode 1: The Sour Receipt

Core

Runtime

Target 150 seconds. Paid test can render the first 45 seconds first.

Hook

Stella holds up a wet receipt and says, "Marco, why is your name on a drink order from before you entered the villa?"

Stakes

The winner of the first challenge gets the Golden Peel bracelet and chooses first at recoupling.

Cliffhanger

The receipt is not signed by Marco or Stella. It is signed by Benny, the host.

Character Motives

Stella Strawberry

Wants to look sweet while controlling the first vote. She knows Marco from before the show, but she does not want the villa to know she helped him rehearse his entrance.

Mango Marco

Wants to be the bombshell everyone chooses. He is not purely fake: he came in liking Stella, then panicked when he realized the pre-show receipt could make both of them look staged.

Bella Blueberry

Looks like gossip, but her real motive is self-protection. She was humiliated in a previous villa and now records every production clue so she never gets blindsided again.

Gina Grape

Knows villa secrets because she arrived during rehearsal day. She lets the others fight until the evidence points toward production itself.

Benny Banana

Comic host who acts harmless. Episode 1 ends by implying he is running a loyalty test on the cast, not just hosting the show.

Full Episode Script

Scene 1 · 0-7s · Cold open

The receipt hits the table

Stella: "Marco, why is your name on a drink order from before you entered the villa?"

Visual: Stella Strawberry slaps a wet smoothie-bar receipt onto a glass table. Mango Marco smiles for half a second, then loses it. Bella Blueberry leans into frame like she has been waiting all day.

Caption: He was not supposed to be here yet.

Scene 2 · 7-16s · Twelve hours earlier

Marco enters too confidently

Narrator: "Twelve hours earlier, Mango Marco walked in like the villa had already voted for him."

Marco: "I do not chase attention. Attention just gets thirsty."

Visual: Marco steps through palm shadows in an open linen shirt. The camera pushes in like a reality-show bombshell entrance. Stella claps too late, like she recognizes him and is trying to hide it.

Caption: Notice Stella's reaction.

Scene 3 · 16-27s · Confessional

Bella clocks the first lie

Bella: "Everyone screamed when Marco came in. Stella did not scream. She counted to two, then screamed. That is not excitement. That is math."

Visual: Bella in a blue-lit confession booth, glossy eyes, tiny mic charm catching light. She taps twice on a notebook labeled villa clicks.

Caption: Bella records everything.

Scene 4 · 27-39s · Challenge setup

The Golden Peel rule

Benny: "First challenge. Make a smoothie for the fruit you trust most. Winner gets the Golden Peel and first pick tonight."

Visual: Benny Banana rolls out a gold bracelet on a velvet tray. The bracelet is shaped like a peel. Everyone wants it, but Stella looks at the bracelet before Benny announces the prize.

Caption: First pick at recoupling.

Scene 5 · 39-51s · Suspicious chemistry

Stella and Marco match too fast

Marco: "Strawberry, lime, and a little salt. That is Stella's drink."

Stella: "How would you know that?"

Marco: "Lucky guess."

Visual: Marco mixes Stella's exact favorite drink without asking. Stella smiles, then hides the smile behind her hand.

Caption: Lucky guess?

Scene 6 · 51-63s · Gina sees the prop

The bracelet already has fingerprints

Gina: "That bracelet has mango pulp on it."

Benny: "Gina, we are flirting, not doing forensics."

Visual: Gina Grape tilts the Golden Peel bracelet toward the sun. A tiny orange smear glows on the clasp. Benny snatches the tray back too fast.

Caption: Why was the prize already touched?

Scene 7 · 63-75s · First public choice

Marco picks Stella and breaks the room

Marco: "My smoothie goes to Stella."

Perry Peach: "You have known her for eight minutes."

Marco: "Some fruit ripens fast."

Visual: The cast laughs except Bella. Stella takes the drink with both hands, almost sentimental, then remembers the cameras.

Caption: The villa turns.

Scene 8 · 75-88s · Bella's door-click clue

Confessional booth timestamp

Bella: "The booth door clicked at 2:13 a.m. Production said nobody was inside. But someone ordered two mango mocktails at 2:16."

Visual: Bella plays a tiny audio recorder. We hear two clicks and a faint laugh. The shot cuts to the empty smoothie bar at night.

Caption: 2:13 a.m. booth click.

Scene 9 · 88-101s · Stella confronts Marco

Private whisper by the pool

Stella: "You said nobody would find the receipt."

Marco: "I said nobody would care if we acted normal."

Visual: They whisper beside the pool while candles flicker. Marco reaches for Stella's wrist, revealing she already has a faint bracelet mark.

Caption: They knew each other.

Scene 10 · 101-114s · The villa ceremony

The evidence goes public

Benny: "Before first pick, any final truths?"

Bella: "Yeah. Ask the smoothie printer."

Visual: Bella raises the wet receipt. The villa goes quiet. Stella does not look at the receipt. She looks at Benny.

Caption: Ask the printer.

Scene 11 · 114-127s · Stella flips the accusation

Stella stops pretending

Stella: "Marco did come early. But he did not invite himself."

Marco: "Stella, don't."

Visual: Stella steps into the center of the ceremony circle. Her sweet smile drops. The camera moves from Stella to Marco to Benny.

Caption: He did not invite himself.

Scene 12 · 127-139s · The receipt reveal

Not Marco's signature

Gina: "Everybody is reading the drink order. Nobody is reading the signature."

Visual: Gina turns the receipt over. There is a signature line. It reads: Benny B.

Caption: BENNY B.

Scene 13 · 139-150s · Loop cliffhanger

Benny smiles

Benny: "Tomorrow night, we find out who came for love... and who came for instructions."

Stella: "Then start with your own."

Visual: Benny's smile holds too long. Marco reaches for the receipt, but Stella pulls it away. Cut as the Golden Peel bracelet drops into the pool.

Caption: Part 2 starts with Benny.

Part 2 Payoff

Part 2 must not dodge the cliffhanger. It opens at the pool with Benny admitting he invited Marco early as a "loyalty test," then reveals Stella agreed because she wanted Perry Peach to show jealousy. The bigger twist: Bella's 2:13 a.m. audio includes Gina's laugh, meaning Gina knew about the test and waited to expose it at the most damaging moment.
