Fruit Crush Villa Episode 1: The Sour Receipt

A detailed multi-minute story lock for the first paid render test. It is deep enough for character motivation and continuity, but still bounded to one episode before spending on a full season.

Back to dashboard | Open JSON story file

Core

Runtime

Target 240 seconds. Paid test should render the first 2-3 strongest scenes before the full episode.

Hook

Stella holds up a wet receipt and says, "Marco, why is your name on a drink order from before you entered the villa?"

Stakes

The winner of the first challenge gets the Golden Peel bracelet, chooses first at recoupling, and earns private access to the locked Juice Room where one production clue is hidden.

Cliffhanger

The receipt is not signed by Marco or Stella. It is signed by Benny, the host, and the back of it says: TEST SUBJECT - STELLA.

Logline

A wet smoothie-bar receipt unravels the first day of Fruit Crush Villa: Marco was inside before his bombshell entrance, Stella helped rehearse the lie, Bella has timestamps, Gina has a rehearsal-day secret, and Benny Banana may have staged the entire fight as a loyalty test.

Act Structure

Act 1 · 0-56

Open with the receipt accusation, then rewind to show Marco's too-polished entrance and Stella's delayed reaction.

Act 2 · 56-132

Turn the challenge into a real game with the Golden Peel, the Juice Room, and visible evidence that Marco touched the prize before anyone else.

Act 3 · 132-204

Let Bella and Gina expose separate clues so the story becomes bigger than Marco and Stella flirting.

Act 4 · 204-240

Flip the target from Marco to Benny, reveal the TEST SUBJECT note, and promise a specific Part 2 answer.

Character Motives

Stella Strawberry

Motive: Look sweet while controlling the first vote.

Secret: She helped Marco rehearse his entrance before the show.

Mango Marco

Motive: Become the bombshell everyone chooses.

Secret: He entered the villa before his official arrival and panicked when the receipt could expose it.

Bella Blueberry

Motive: Protect herself by noticing every production clue.

Secret: She records every confessional door click after being blindsided in a previous villa.

Gina Grape

Motive: Expose who came for love and who came for screen time.

Secret: She arrived during rehearsal day and heard more than she admits.

Benny Banana

Motive: Run the villa like a comic game master while hiding producer-level influence.

Secret: He signed the 2:16 a.m. smoothie receipt.

Continuity Rules

  • Part 2 must answer why Benny signed the receipt before introducing a new twist.
  • Stella cannot be purely innocent; she rehearsed with Marco, but she may not know she was the test subject.
  • Marco cannot be purely fake; he likes Stella and panics because the receipt makes his real feelings look produced.
  • Bella must remain useful, not just noisy: every accusation needs a timestamp, audio, or physical clue.
  • Gina knows more than she says because she arrived on rehearsal day.

Full Episode Script

Scene 1 · 0-8s · Cold open

Cold open

Stella: Marco, why is your name on a drink order from before you entered the villa?

Visual: Stella Strawberry slaps a wet smoothie-bar receipt onto a glass table. Mango Marco smiles for half a second, then loses it. Bella Blueberry leans into frame.

Caption: He was inside before the villa opened.

Scene 2 · 8-20s · Twelve hours earlier

Twelve hours earlier

Narrator: Twelve hours earlier, Mango Marco walked in like the villa had already voted for him. The weird part was not his confidence. It was Stella's face.

Marco: I do not chase attention. Attention just gets thirsty.

Visual: Marco steps through palm shadows in an open linen shirt. Everyone cheers on instinct. Stella freezes, looks at the smoothie bar, then claps two beats late.

Caption: Notice Stella's reaction.

Scene 3 · 20-32s · Bella confessional

Bella confessional

Bella: Everyone screamed when Marco came in. Stella did not scream. She looked at the bar first. Then she counted to two. Then she screamed. That is not excitement. That is math.

Visual: Bella sits in a blue-lit confession booth and taps twice on a notebook labeled villa clicks.

Caption: Bella records everything.

Scene 4 · 32-44s · Golden Peel rule

Golden Peel rule

Benny: First challenge. Make a smoothie for the fruit you trust most. Winner gets the Golden Peel, first pick tonight, and ten minutes in the locked Juice Room.

Visual: Benny rolls out a gold bracelet on a velvet tray and taps a locked door behind the bar. Stella looks at the door before he explains it.

Caption: First pick. Locked room. One clue.

Scene 5 · 44-56s · Suspicious chemistry

Suspicious chemistry

Marco: Strawberry, lime, and a little salt. That is Stella's drink.

Stella: How would you know that?

Marco: Lucky guess.

Visual: Marco mixes Stella's exact favorite drink without asking. Stella smiles, then hides the smile behind her hand.

Caption: Lucky guess?

Scene 6 · 56-68s · Gina sees the prop

Gina sees the prop

Gina: That bracelet has mango pulp on it.

Benny: Gina, we are flirting, not doing forensics.

Visual: Gina tilts the Golden Peel bracelet toward the sun. A tiny orange smear glows on the clasp. Benny snatches the tray back too fast.

Caption: Why was the prize already touched?

Scene 7 · 68-80s · First public choice

First public choice

Marco: My smoothie goes to Stella.

Perry: You have known her for eight minutes.

Marco: Some fruit ripens fast.

Visual: Marco gives Stella the smoothie. The cast laughs except Bella. Stella takes the drink with both hands, almost sentimental, then remembers the cameras.

Caption: The villa turns.

Scene 8 · 80-92s · Perry gets hurt

Perry gets hurt

Perry: I told Stella my drink on the ride in. Mango somehow knew it too.

Stella: Perry, I did not tell him that.

Perry: You did not have to. Someone did.

Visual: Perry Peach steps forward holding a half-made peach-ginger smoothie. Stella turns defensive. Marco looks down at the blender like it betrayed him.

Caption: Marco knew Perry's drink too.

Scene 9 · 92-104s · Marco private confessional

Marco private confessional

Marco: I was told one thing. Come in strong. Pick Stella. Do not mention last night. I thought that was normal bombshell stuff. Now everyone is looking at me like I wrote the script.

Visual: Marco sits alone in the orange confessional booth. His charm drops. He rubs his necklace and glances toward the door before saying last night.

Caption: Marco says he was coached.

Scene 10 · 104-118s · Bella's timestamp clue

Bella's timestamp clue

Bella: The booth door clicked at 2:13 a.m. Production said nobody was inside. But someone ordered two mango mocktails at 2:16.

Benny: Bella, maybe the printer was lonely.

Bella: Then why did it print two straws?

Visual: Bella plays a tiny audio recorder. We hear two clicks and a faint laugh. She lays down a straw wrapper beside the receipt.

Caption: 2:13 a.m. booth click.

Scene 11 · 118-132s · Private whisper

Private whisper

Stella: You said nobody would find the receipt.

Marco: I said nobody would care if we acted normal.

Visual: Stella and Marco whisper beside the pool. Marco reaches for Stella's wrist, revealing a faint bracelet mark. Stella pulls her hand back and looks toward the locked Juice Room.

Caption: They knew each other.

Scene 12 · 132-144s · Gina rehearsal-day memory

Gina rehearsal-day memory

Gina: Funny. When I arrived for wardrobe yesterday, that Juice Room was not locked.

Benny: Gina, rehearsal day is not canon.

Gina: Receipts are.

Visual: Gina Grape steps from the lounge shadows and points at the Juice Room. Benny's host smile twitches when she says wardrobe yesterday.

Caption: Gina was here yesterday.

Scene 13 · 144-156s · Juice Room entry

Juice Room entry

Stella: If the bracelet opens that room, give it to me.

Perry: You have not won.

Stella: No. But I have already been inside.

Visual: Stella reaches for the Golden Peel bracelet. The villa goes silent. Perry steps back like the sentence hurt more than the lie.

Caption: Stella admits she was inside.

Scene 14 · 156-168s · The locked drawer

The locked drawer

Narrator: Inside the Juice Room, the cameras found exactly one thing that did not belong to any contestant.

Bella: A printer log.

Gina: And there is Benny's name again.

Visual: A drawer opens under the smoothie printer. Bella pulls out a damp printer log while Gina blocks Benny from stepping closer.

Caption: The printer log has Benny's name.

Scene 15 · 168-180s · Stella reveals her reason

Stella reveals her reason

Stella: I practiced with Marco because I wanted one person here who would not make me beg to be picked.

Perry: You could have just told me.

Stella: I was scared you would believe the sweet version of me more than the real one.

Visual: Stella stands between Marco and Perry. Her confident posture breaks for the first time. Marco stops trying to defend himself and listens.

Caption: Stella's lie had a reason.

Scene 16 · 180-192s · Benny redirects blame

Benny redirects blame

Benny: Beautiful honesty. Messy, fruity, legally not my problem.

Bella: You signed the receipt.

Benny: I sign many things. Photos. Waivers. Emotional damage.

Visual: Benny laughs too loudly and lifts both hands like a harmless host. Bella does not blink. The cast stops laughing one by one.

Caption: Benny jokes when cornered.

Scene 17 · 192-204s · Villa ceremony evidence

Villa ceremony evidence

Benny: Before first pick, any final truths?

Bella: Yeah. Ask the smoothie printer.

Visual: Bella raises the wet receipt beside the printer log. The villa goes quiet. Stella looks at Benny instead of the receipt.

Caption: Ask the printer.

Scene 18 · 204-216s · Stella flips the accusation

Stella flips the accusation

Stella: Marco did come early. But he did not invite himself.

Marco: Stella, don't.

Visual: Stella steps into the ceremony circle. Her sweet smile drops. The camera moves from Stella to Marco to Benny. Marco nods once, finally choosing truth over charm.

Caption: He did not invite himself.

Scene 19 · 216-228s · Receipt signature

Receipt signature

Gina: Everybody is reading the drink order. Nobody is reading the signature.

Visual: Gina turns the receipt over. The signature line reads Benny B. Under it, in smudged red ink, it says TEST SUBJECT - STELLA.

Caption: BENNY B. / TEST SUBJECT - STELLA

Scene 20 · 228-240s · Loop cliffhanger

Loop cliffhanger

Benny: Tomorrow night, we find out who came for love... and who came with instructions.

Stella: No. Part 2 starts with who made me the test.

Visual: Benny smiles too long. Marco reaches for the receipt, Stella pulls it away, and the Golden Peel bracelet drops into the pool.

Caption: Part 2 starts with Benny.

Part 2 Payoff

Part 2 must open on the pool, not a recap. Benny admits he invited Marco early as a loyalty test, but claims Stella volunteered because she wanted to see whether Perry Peach would get jealous. Stella denies volunteering. Bella then plays the full 2:13 a.m. booth audio, where Gina laughs and says, 'Do not tell Stella what Benny promised Marco.' Episode 2 resolves Benny's test before adding the next mystery.
