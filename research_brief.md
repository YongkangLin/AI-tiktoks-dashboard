# AI TikTok Monetization Research Brief

As of May 30, 2026, the working conclusion is: AI-generated TikToks are not automatically demonetized, but low-quality, repetitive, unoriginal, misleading, unlabeled, or rights-risky AI content is exposed to disqualification, removal, or account review.

## Official Policy Baseline

TikTok Creator Rewards focuses on original, high-quality videos that are at least one minute long and comply with TikTok policies. Rewards depend on qualified views and RPM rather than total public views alone.

Official references:

- https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-rewards-program/creator-rewards-program
- https://support.tiktok.com/en/business-and-creator/creator-rewards-program/how-rewards-work/
- https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/global/creator-rewards-program-us/en
- https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/global/tiktok-creator-rewards-program-eea/en

TikTok's AI-generated content guidance allows AI creativity, but requires labels for realistic AI-generated or significantly edited people/scenes and prohibits harmful impersonation, misleading public-interest content, and unauthorized likeness use.

Official references:

- https://support.tiktok.com/en/using-tiktok/creating-videos/ai-generated-content
- https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en-GB/integrity-authenticity?cgversion=2025H2update

## Market Signal

AI microdrama has clear attention potential. Public reporting around Fruit Love Island described rapid audience growth into millions of followers and hundreds of millions of views, followed by takedowns, criticism, copycat pressure, and platform uncertainty.

The lesson is not to clone that show. The defensible strategy is to build original serialized IP with clear authorship, consistent characters, fresh scenes, and proof of production.

## Practical Standard For This Project

- Original show titles, characters, locations, and hashtags.
- 61+ second runtime target.
- Distinct scene actions instead of loops or slideshows.
- AI disclosure plan at upload.
- Original, licensed, or platform-cleared music.
- No celebrity likeness, private-person likeness, minors, logos, copyrighted characters, or protected franchise terms.
- No bought views, engagement pods, automated engagement, Duets, Stitches, or reposted third-party footage.
- Cross-post to reduce dependence on one platform's opaque moderation and RPM.
