# Duanju Story Model

Use Duanju as the pacing model for Fruit Crush Villa and other AI drama series.

## What We Are Borrowing

Duanju is Chinese vertical micro-drama: short mobile-first episodes, serialized across many installments, built around relentless hooks, reversals, and cliffhangers. The useful lesson is structural, not cultural copying.

Sources reviewed:

- Screenburn, "Microdrama Structure": https://screenburn.app/learn/microdrama-structure
  - Useful rule: microdrama episodes use a hook, one escalation movement, and a cliffhanger; series usually span 60-100 episodes of 1-3 minutes.
- Yale Journal of International Affairs, "Micro-Drama as Soft Power": https://www.yalejournal.org/publications/micro-drama-as-soft-power-yedbr
  - Useful rule: Duanju episodes are commonly 60-90 seconds, mobile-first, and cliffhangers function as the conversion prompt.
- Global Media and China / Sage, "Everybody Is Moving From Long Form to Short Form": https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/pdf/10.1177/20594364261440574?download=true
  - Useful rule: for one-minute episodes, the first and last two or three seconds are decisive; every word has to be efficient.
- Le Monde, "Chinese Hollywood races to cash in on minidramas boom": https://www.lemonde.fr/en/m-le-mag/article/2025/08/17/chinese-hollywood-races-to-cash-in-on-the-minidramas-boom_6744449_117.html
  - Useful rule: producers describe two major plot twists per episode, dense editing, and no shot lasting more than about five seconds.

## Rules For Our Scripts

- Open mid-conflict. A slap, receipt, accusation, secret text, betrayal, or exposed prop must appear in the first 3 seconds.
- One episode equals one dramatic movement. Do not introduce five unrelated mysteries.
- Use two reversals per minute: one mid-episode turn and one final turn.
- Keep shots under 5 seconds unless the character is actively reacting.
- Favor two-person confrontations and close-ups. Bring extra characters in through reactions, overheard audio, texts, receipts, or door clicks.
- Verbalize emotional stakes fast. Duanju characters say the subtext because there is no time for slow inference.
- End on a specific unanswered question, not a vague teaser.
- The next episode must answer the previous cliffhanger before adding a new cliffhanger.

## Fruit Crush Villa Adaptation

The Duanju engine becomes:

1. Cold proof: a physical clue appears immediately.
2. Emotional accusation: one character says what the clue means.
3. Flashback or reveal: show the audience what the villa missed.
4. Midpoint reversal: the accused person is not the only liar.
5. Public confrontation: the social order changes in front of everyone.
6. Final reversal: the host or hidden rule changes the meaning of the clue.
7. Loop cut: end mid-answer or mid-reaction so the video snaps back into the opening.

## Paid Video Implication

Every paid generation prompt should be designed around one sharp close-up action: receipt slap, eye dart, bracelet smear, door click, phone screen, drink handoff, mouth opening to confess, or host smile dropping. If the shot cannot be understood on mute in under one second, rewrite it before rendering.
