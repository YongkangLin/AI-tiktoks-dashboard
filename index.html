The promise is a lie, but the marriage reveals her mother's hidden trust, a covered contract clause, and a public gala trap she can turn against the family that used her. Prompt Rules

Global lookVertical 9:16 cinematic Chinese Duanju, realistic wealthy-family melodrama, luxury wedding, hospital, mansion, garage, gala.
MotionOne clear action per shot: document reveal, hidden recording, public humiliation, signature reversal, legal proof, DNA cliffhanger.

Text handlingDo not ask the video model to render tiny legal text. Add clauses, report names, captions, and dates in edit.
