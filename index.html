One-Minute Script

Bananita has five dollars, an eviction notice, and a girlfriend who calls him broke. A convenience-store clerk tosses him a scratched ticket as a joke. The ticket wins one million dollars, but the final receipt suggests the ticket may have been bought with Strawbella's card.

Prompt Rules

Global lookVertical 9:16 gritty cinematic 3D fruit drama: apartment, wet street, convenience store, eviction door, news van.
MotionOne clear action per shot: insult, choice, tossed ticket, scan, win, eviction, betrayal, news arrival, receipt reveal.
ContinuityKeep Bananita's hoodie, silver chain, ripped jeans, anxious charcoal-banana face, and scale consistent.
Text handlingDo not ask the video model to render readable text. Add captions, dollar amount, scanner result, and receipt text in edit.
AvoidLong dialogue scenes, bright mascot comedy, photoreal humans, watermarks, random extra characters, fake readable screen text.
